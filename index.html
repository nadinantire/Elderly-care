Our programs and services are here to support you and your family

Agaciro Elderly Care Rwanda (AEC) provides comprehensive support for elderly individuals through nutrition assistance, social engagement activities, and accessible healthcare services to promote health, dignity, and community connection.

Nutrition and Food Security

Food distribution includes nutritious supplies, kitchen gardens for the elderly, essential supplements, and nutrition workshops focused on traditional Rwandan practices.

Social Engagement

AEC fosters social engagement for the elderly through gatherings, intergenerational storytelling, and community service.

Health Services and Insurance Support

The program provides insurance support, access to medical providers, and mobile health clinics for the elderly.

Who We Are

Agaciro Elderly Care Rwanda (AEC) is committed to improving the lives of elders in Rwanda. Grounded in agaciro (dignity) and ubumuntu (humanity), we provide care and wellness programs that honor our elders.

Together, through umuganda (community collaboration), we work For the well-being of generations.

  • Enhance Nutrition and Food Security.
  • Foster Social Engagement.
  • mprove Access to Health Services.
  • Advocate for Elderly Rights and Needs.
